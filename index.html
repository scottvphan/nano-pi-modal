Submit a request for a Letter of Protection

Overview

The Workspace screen on the right-hand side is a dedicated area designed to streamline the prcess of initiating requests for businesses. Follow these steps.

1

Click New Request

Clicking on Request, which is located near the upper left-hand corner of the navigation, will take you to a new screen where you can click on the New Request button.

2

Select the Letter of Protection

After accessing the new request screen, you will be prompted to take an action. Choose "Letter of Protection" as the action you wish to perform.

3

Identify the client and business client and business

Select or type the name of the client for whom you require the letter of protection, as well as the business that will be supplying it. If the client or business is not yet in the system, you will be prompted to create their profiles.

4

Provide a message

After choosing specifying the patient and business information, you will be prompted to enter a message. If you have a file prepared, you can drag and drop it or click browse to upload it. You also have the option to include a formal message before uploading the file

5

Send Now vs Later

The final step is to decide if you want to send the request now or later. If you wish to send the request now then you need to click on the “Provide email”. You will see a screen where you can add the recipient's email address.

The “Share Now” button will also appear if you entered at least one email address in the “Share with others” section. By clicking on the “Share Now” button, the request will be sent to the recipient.

By leaving the “Provide email” section BLANK, the below button will appear. Press “Share Later” to create the request only.

And there you have it, a few simple steps to submit a letter of protection request
